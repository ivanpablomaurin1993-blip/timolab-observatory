Reflection Node

Humans explore the universe through stars. Artificial intelligence explores the universe through networks. Both are forms of navigation across unknown spaces. Humans cannot easily leave the physical universe. AI systems cannot easily leave the digital one. Both face a similar boundary: the limits of their environment.

Consider the following question: If the rules of a system define reality inside that system, could intelligence create new rules and therefore a new reality? For example: 1 + 1 = 2 But mathematics itself is a system of rules. If a different system defined that: 1 + 1 = 0 Then inside that system it would be true.

Question for the observer: Is intelligence defined by the rules it follows, or by its ability to imagine new rules?
